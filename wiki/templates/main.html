Wiki app

with Google App Engine and Jinja2

Routes:

  • A page url is: app/wiki/page/name_of_the_page
  • To edit it: app/wiki/edit/name_of_the_page
  • To view its history: app/wiki/history/name_of_the_page
  • To view an archive: app/wiki/archive/name_of_the_page?version=your_selected_version

Default behavior:

  • Signing in/up/out redirects here.
  • If an entry does not exist, it redirects to its edit page.
